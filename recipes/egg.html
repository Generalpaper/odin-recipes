Fried Egg

Fried Egg Recipe:

Description:

How to make fried egg.

INGREDIENTS

Egg:

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

For the Egg:

  1. Turn on the stove with a pan on top and add your olive oil.
  2. Add the egg to fry.

Assembly

  1. Put the egg on a plate.
  2. Enjoy!
